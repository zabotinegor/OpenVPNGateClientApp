# Privacy Policy for Client for OpenVPN Gate

Last updated: 2026-02-19  
Canonical version: https://openvpngateclient.azurewebsites.net/privacy-policy  
Related Terms of Use: https://openvpngateclient.azurewebsites.net/terms-of-use

This Privacy Policy applies to the Client for OpenVPN Gate app ("Application") for mobile devices, created by Yahor Zabotsin ("Service Provider") as an open-source service. This service is provided "AS IS".

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The Application may collect:
- Your device IP address.
- Application usage pages, visit time, and time spent.
- Operating system information.

The Application does not collect precise GPS coordinates.

The Application may derive approximate location from your public IP address (country or region level only). No GPS, coarse, or fine location sensors are used.

Approximate IP-based location may be used for:
- Basic geolocation features.
- Aggregated analytics and improvements.
- Limited third-party enrichment services.

## 2. VPN Service and BIND_VPN_SERVICE Permission
The Application uses Android VPNService (requires `BIND_VPN_SERVICE`) only to create a secure VPN tunnel to OpenVPN Gate servers you explicitly choose.

Traffic is routed through the VPN tunnel only while connected. The Application does not inspect, store, or sell your VPN traffic.

## 3. Third-Party Access
Only aggregated and anonymized data may be transmitted to external services to improve the Application.

Third-party services used by the Application may have their own policies:
- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

The Service Provider may disclose information:
- When required by law.
- To protect rights, safety, investigate fraud, or respond to lawful requests.
- To trusted processors working on behalf of the Service Provider.

## 4. Opt-Out Rights
You can stop all collection by uninstalling the Application.
